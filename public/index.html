About Me

Hi, I'm John Kono. Welcome to my page! Here you can find more about me and my passions. I am studying in BSc Film & Visual Culture at Aberderdeen university, combining my education with my passion for storytelling to create meaningful films. The world of filmmaking is where I've found my truest self a place where imagination meets reality, and stories come alive. My journey as a filmmaker has been a winding road of discovery, passion, and perseverance.

"My dream is to become a filmmaker, director, and cinematographer crafting compelling narratives and breathtaking visuals that leave a lasting impact."
